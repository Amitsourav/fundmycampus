## 1. What is FundMyCampus?

FundMyCampus is an **education loan platform** that helps Indian students get loans for studying abroad or in India. We work with **12+ banking partners** (ICICI, Axis, Credila, SBI, HDFC, etc.) and have helped **15,000+ students**.

**The core idea is simple:**

> A student fills one application. We match them with the best banks. Our counselors guide them through the entire process until money is disbursed.

**Key numbers:**
- Collateral-free loans starting at **6.75% interest**
- Loans up to **1.5 Crore** (for abroad) and **40 Lakhs** (for India)
- **48-hour** approval turnaround
- **12+ banking partners**

## 2. How Students Sign Up

The signup is a **4-step guided journey**. Students don't see a long boring form — instead, they go through it step by step with friendly animated characters on the screen.

### Step-by-Step Breakdown

**STEP 1 — Create Account**
- Email address
- Password
- Referral code (if someone referred them)
- Consent to be contacted

**STEP 2 — Basic Details**
- Full name
- Phone number
- Gender
- WhatsApp number

**STEP 3 — Education Details**
- Do you have an offer letter?
- Course type (UG / PG / PhD)
- Degree name (B.Tech, MBA...)
- Which country?
- Which college?
- When does the course start?

**STEP 4 — Financial Details**
- How much loan do you need?
- Do you have property to offer as security?
- Co-applicant's monthly income
- Any existing monthly payments?
- How did you hear about us?

**After Step 4 — Application is submitted and the student is taken to their Dashboard.**

**Important notes:**
- Steps 3 and 4 can be **skipped** and filled later from the dashboard
- The left side of the screen shows **animated animal characters** (bear, cat, owl, bunny) that react to what the student is doing — this makes the process feel friendly, not overwhelming
- A **progress bar** at the top shows which step they're on

## 3. How Students Log In

- Email
- Password
- "Remember me" option (stays logged in for 30 days)

**Result:**
- Success — Goes to Dashboard
- Wrong password — Error message shown

**Fun design detail:** The login screen has **animated geometric characters** on the left side. Their eyes follow the student's cursor. When the student types the password, the characters look away (for "privacy"). When the student clicks "show password", one character peeks. **Other account features:**
- **Forgot Password:** Student enters email, receives a reset link, sets new password
- **Phone Verification:** After signup, students can verify their phone number with a one-time code (OTP) sent via SMS

## 5. How a Loan Application Moves from Student to Admin to Bank

This is the **complete journey** of a loan application — from the moment a student fills the form to the money being disbursed.

### Part A: What Happens on the Student Side (Website)

**Step 1.** Student signs up and fills all 4 steps (or fills contact form / starts guest chat)

**Step 2.** Application is created with a unique ID (e.g., FMC-20260327-00142)

**Step 3.** A counselor is automatically assigned (system picks the counselor with fewest active cases)

**Step 4.** Student gets a welcome notification and can use the chatbot for common questions

---

### Part B: What Happens on the Admin / Counselor Side

**Step 5.** Counselor sees the new application in their queue
- Student's full details are visible
- Education info, loan amount, collateral status — everything is there

**Step 6.** Counselor reviews and chats with the student
- Asks for missing documents
- Clarifies loan requirements
- Guides on which banks are best for their case

**Step 7.** Counselor ADDS BANKS to the application
- Can use "Match Bank Offers" to auto-find best banks
- Or manually add banks (e.g., ICICI + Axis + Credila)
- Each bank gets its own progress tracker
- Student can see all banks processing their loan on their dashboard

**Step 8.** Document Review
- Student uploads documents from their dashboard
- Documents appear in counselor's review queue
- Counselor can preview each document
- Can VERIFY (approved) or REJECT (with reason shown to student)
- Student gets a notification for each review result

**Step 9.** Counselor updates bank-wise status as loan progresses
- Moves each bank through the 7 stages: Applied, Docs Received, Under Review, Loan Login, Sanctioned, Processing Fee, Disbursed
- Can add remarks/notes at each stage
- Student sees live updates on their dashboard
- The main application status auto-updates to the best progress across all banks

**Step 10.** Final outcome:
- **DISBURSED** — Money sent to university!
- **REJECTED** — Reason shared with student
- **WITHDRAWN** — Student chose to cancel

## 7. Chat System — How Conversations Work End-to-End

The chat system has **2 modes** depending on whether the visitor has an account or not:

### Mode 1: Guest Chat (No signup needed)

This is for **website visitors** who haven't created an account yet.

**Step 1.** Visitor clicks the chat bubble on any page

**Step 2.** Simple form appears asking for Name and Phone number

**Step 3.** Chat opens with automated bot. Bot says welcome message and shows quick reply buttons (e.g., "Study Abroad Loan", "Loan Eligibility", "Talk to Expert")

**Step 4.** Visitor types a question
- System searches FAQ database for matching answer
- If match found: shows answer + more quick replies
- If no match: shows fallback message with contact info and option to talk to a real person

**Step 5.** If visitor clicks "Talk to Expert"
- System shows contact details (phone, email) so the visitor can reach the team directly

**Behind the scenes:**
- Guest gets a token (valid for 30 days) saved in their browser
- If they come back within 30 days, chat history is restored
- All conversations are visible in Admin panel under Guest Chats
- The page where chat started is logged (e.g., /tools, /abroad-study-loan)

---

### Mode 2: Bot Chat (Logged-in student)

After logging in, students can chat with the same automated bot from their dashboard.

**Step 1.** Student clicks chat in their dashboard

**Step 2.** Bot answers from FAQ database. Same matching system as guest chat. Shows quick reply buttons after each answer.

**Step 3.** If the bot can't answer, it provides contact details (phone, email) so the student can reach the team directly.

**Note:** Currently, the chat system is **bot-only** (automated FAQ responses). There is no live counselor chat feature on the student website. All human communication between counselors and students happens via phone, email, or WhatsApp outside the platform. The admin panel has chat infrastructure ready for a future live chat feature.

---

## 8. Referral Program — How Students Earn by Sharing

Every registered student gets a **unique referral code** and a **shareable link** (e.g., fundmycampus.com/signup?ref=ABC123).

### How It Works

**Step 1.** Student shares their referral link with a friend

**Step 2.** Friend signs up using that link
- Student gets notified
- Referral status: "Signed Up"

**Step 3.** Friend submits loan application
- Referral status: "Applied"

**Step 4.** Friend's loan gets SANCTIONED (approved by bank)
- Student earns 1,000
- BONUS: Every 10th successful referral = 5,000
- Referral status: "Sanctioned"

**Step 5.** Friend's loan gets DISBURSED (money sent)
- Student earns another 1,000
- Friend ALSO gets 1,000 bonus!
- Referral status: "Disbursed"

**Note:** Minimum loan amount must be 10 Lakhs to qualify.

### Earning Summary

| Event | What the referrer earns |
|-------|------------------------|
| Friend's loan sanctioned | 1,000 |
| Friend's loan disbursed | 1,000 |
| Every 10th successful referral | 5,000 milestone bonus |
| **Total per referral** | **2,000 + bonuses** |

### What Students See in the Referral Dashboard

- Their **unique referral code** with a one-click copy button
- Their **shareable link** with a one-click copy button
- **Referral history** — list of all friends who signed up using their code, with status (signed up / applied / sanctioned / disbursed)
- **Payout history** — all earnings with amount, type, date, and payout status (completed / processing / pending)

### How Admin Processes Referral Payouts

1. Counselor sees referral status update in admin panel
2. Clicks "Process" to advance the referral
3. System auto-calculates the payout amount (including milestone bonuses)
4. For actual money transfer: enters student's bank details (account number, IFSC code, account holder name)
5. Payment is processed via **Razorpay** (payment gateway)
6. Student sees payout status update: Pending, Processing, Completed

## 9. Free Tools — Calculators and Planning

We offer **6 free tools** that help students plan their education financing. These tools work instantly — no login required, no data is sent anywhere. Everything calculates on the student's own device.

### Tool 1: EMI Calculator
**What it does:** Shows how much monthly payment (EMI) a student will have to pay

**Student inputs:**
- Loan amount (1 Lakh to 1 Crore)
- Interest rate (% per year)
- Loan duration (1 to 20 years)

**Student sees:**
- Monthly EMI amount
- Total interest they'll pay over the loan period
- Total amount payable (loan + interest)

---

### Tool 2: Education Cost Calculator
**What it does:** Estimates the **total cost** of studying at a specific college, including tuition, living, and other expenses

**Student inputs:**
- Country (India, USA, UK, Canada, Australia)
- Course type (Engineering, Medical, Management, Arts, Science)
- College name (100+ colleges pre-loaded with actual fee data)
- Duration of study (1-6 years)
- Accommodation type (Hostel or Private)

**Student sees:**
- Tuition fee (total for full duration)
- Living expenses
- Other expenses (books, insurance, travel, etc.)
- **Grand total cost**

**What makes this special:** We have **real fee data** for 100+ colleges across 5 countries — IITs, IIMs, AIIMS, MIT, Stanford, Oxford, Cambridge, and many more. Students get realistic estimates, not guesses.

---

### Tool 3: Loan Eligibility Checker
**What it does:** Tells students the **maximum loan amount** they can get based on their financial situation

**Student inputs:**
- Their monthly income
- Co-applicant's monthly income (parent/guardian)
- Existing monthly payments (other loans/EMIs)
- Credit score range

**Student sees:**
- Maximum eligible loan amount
- Maximum affordable monthly payment
- **Bank-wise offers** from 4 banks (SBI, HDFC, ICICI, Axis) showing max loan each bank would offer and interest rate range

---

### Tool 4: Loan Comparison Tool
**What it does:** Lets students compare **3 different loan offers** side by side to find the cheapest one

**Student inputs (for each of 3 loans):**
- Loan amount
- Interest rate
- Duration
- Processing fee

**Student sees:**
- Monthly EMI for each loan
- Total interest for each loan
- Total cost (including processing fee) for each loan
- **"Best Option" badge** on the cheapest loan

---

### Tool 5: Tax Benefit Calculator
**What it does:** Shows how much **income tax** a student (or their family) can save under Section 80E (government tax benefit on education loan interest)

**Student inputs:**
- Annual interest paid on the loan
- Income tax bracket (10%, 20%, or 30%)

**Student sees:**
- Annual tax savings
- Total savings over the full loan period (10 years)
- Effective interest rate (after tax benefit — always lower than the bank's rate)

---

### Tool 6: SOP Review Tool
**What it does:** Reviews a student's **Statement of Purpose** (SOP — the essay required for university admissions) and gives a score with feedback

**Student inputs:**
- Course type (MBA, Masters, PhD, Undergraduate)
- Target country
- Their SOP text (paste or type)

**Student sees:**
- **Overall score** out of 100
- **Strengths** found in the SOP
- **Areas to improve** with specific suggestions
- Word count analysis (ideal: 500-1000 words)
- Readability level (simple / good / complex)

**Scoring checks for:**
- Personal motivation and passion
- Clear career goals
- Why this specific program/university
- Relevant experience and internships
- Skills mentioned

## 10. What Makes FundMyCampus Unique

### 10.1 One Application, Multiple Banks
Most platforms connect students to just one bank. **We send their application to multiple banks simultaneously** and let the student pick the best offer. The student fills the form once — we handle the rest with 12+ banking partners.

### 10.2 Real-Time Loan Tracking (Like Order Tracking)
Just like tracking a delivery, students can see **exactly where their loan is** with each bank — in a clear 7-step visual tracker. No guessing, no calling to ask "what's the status?"

### 10.3 Dedicated Counselor for Every Student
